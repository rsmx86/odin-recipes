Chicken & Rice

45 min • Easy / Fácil

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cubed (peitos de frango em cubos)
  • 1 cup white rice (xícara de arroz branco)
  • 2 cups hot chicken broth (caldo de galinha quente)
  • 1 onion & 2 garlic cloves, minced (cebola e alho picados)
  • Olive oil, salt, and turmeric (azeite, sal e cúrcuma)

Preparation / Preparo

  1. 01. **Searing:** Season the chicken and sear it in olive oil until golden. Remove and set aside.
    *(Sele o frango temperado no azeite até dourar. Retire e reserve.)*
  2. 02. **Aromatics:** In the same pan, sauté the onion and garlic until translucent to capture the chicken flavor.
    *(Na mesma panela, refogue a cebola e o alho até ficarem transparentes.)*
  3. 03. **Toasting:** Add the rice and toast it for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the turmeric.
    *(Adicione o arroz e frite por 2 minutos, mexendo sempre. Adicione a cúrcuma.)*
  4. 04. **Simmering:** Pour the hot broth, return the chicken to the pan, and season with salt.
    *(Despeje o caldo quente, volte o frango para a panela e ajuste o sal.)*
  5. 05. **Finishing:** Cook on low heat with a semi-closed lid until the water is absorbed. Let it rest for 5 min before serving.
    *(Cozinhe em fogo baixo com a tampa entreaberta. Deixe descansar 5 min antes de servir.)*
