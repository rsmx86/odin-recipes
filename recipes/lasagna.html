Classic Lasagna

60 min — Medium Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 500g lasagna pasta
  • 500g ground beef

Preparation Method

  1. 01. Prepare the Bolognese sauce by cooking the meat with spices and tomato sauce.
  2. 02. Put the lasagna pasta layer by layer on a pasta dish with the Bolognse sauce in each layer post.
